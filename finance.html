Scholarship Benefits

Recipients of the cube Innovation hub Scholarships not only receive financial assistance but also:

  1. Access to expert mentorship from industry leaders
  2. Inclusion in a collaborative community of inovators and tech enthusiasts
  3. A tailored learning path that aligns with career aspirations.
  4. Real-word project exposure through practical training

Application process

To apply for the scholarships, follow this steps:

  1. Visit our scholarship page
  2. Fill in the application form, including your academic background and personal statement
  3. Submit any supporting documents, such as financial statements for need-based scholarships
  4. Await confirmation and follow-up for any additional interviews requirements.
